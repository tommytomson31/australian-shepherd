How It All Began

A Love That Started With One Aussie

Over fifteen years ago, our family welcomed our very first Australian Shepherd into our home. We had no plans to become breeders. We simply fell in love with the intelligence, loyalty, and boundless energy that defines this remarkable breed. That first Aussie changed everything for us. She became our constant companion, our children's best friend, and the spark that ignited a lifelong passion.

What started as a single dog quickly grew into something much larger. Friends and family couldn't help but notice the incredible temperament and beauty of our Aussies, and before long, we were fielding requests from people who wanted a puppy just like ours. We knew that if we were going to bring more of these wonderful dogs into the world, we would do it the right way.

Heritage Hill Aussies was founded on one unwavering principle: every single dog in our care is a member of our family first. We are not a kennel. We are not a puppy mill. We are a family who shares our home, our lives, and our hearts with our dogs. Every breeding decision we make is guided by a deep respect for the breed standard, a commitment to genetic health, and a genuine love for each individual dog.

Today, our breeding program is recognized for producing Australian Shepherds of exceptional quality, temperament, and health. We take immense pride in the families we've matched with our puppies over the years, and we remain dedicated to raising each litter with the same care and devotion that started this journey.

What We Do

Our Puppy Raising Process

From the moment our puppies are born, they receive around-the-clock care designed to prepare them for a lifetime of happiness in their forever homes.

01

Early Socialization

From birth, our puppies are gently handled and socialized with children, adults, and other pets. This early exposure builds confidence and helps develop well-rounded, friendly temperaments that make the transition to a new home seamless.

02

Environmental Enrichment

Our puppies grow up in our home, surrounded by everyday household sounds and environments. Vacuum cleaners, doorbells, television, kitchen activity, and outdoor play all become part of their normal routine, ensuring nothing phases them in their new homes.

03

Basic Training Foundation

Before leaving Heritage Hill, each puppy receives foundational potty training and exposure to crate training. We begin introducing basic routines so your new puppy arrives with a head start on good habits.

04

Comprehensive Veterinary Care

Every puppy follows an age-appropriate vaccination schedule and receives regular veterinary checkups throughout their time with us. You'll receive a complete medical history and documentation when you welcome your puppy home.

05

AKC Registration

Every Heritage Hill Aussie comes with full AKC registration, ensuring documented lineage and breed authenticity. We maintain meticulous records of each puppy's pedigree and provide all registration paperwork at the time of adoption.

06

Temperament Matching

We conduct individual temperament evaluations for each puppy, allowing us to thoughtfully match them with the right families. Whether you're looking for an active hiking companion or a gentle family dog, we'll help you find your perfect match.

Beyond Adoption Day

Our Lifetime Commitment to You

When you adopt a puppy from Heritage Hill Aussies, you're not just bringing home a dog. You're gaining a lifelong resource and a dedicated partner in your journey as a pet owner. Our relationship with your family doesn't end when you drive away with your new puppy. It's only just beginning.

We are here for you every step of the way, from the exciting first days to the golden senior years. Our ongoing support includes:

  • Personalized nutrition guidance tailored to your Aussie's age and activity level
  • Training tips and recommendations from our years of experience with the breed
  • Answers to any health questions or concerns that arise throughout your dog's life
  • Behavioral advice for common challenges and life transitions
  • Grooming recommendations and coat care guidance

We're always just a phone call or email away. Heritage Hill families are part of our extended family, and we genuinely care about the well-being of every puppy we place. Don't hesitate to reach out, whether it's day one or year ten.

Quality You Can Trust

AKC Certification & Health Standards

We uphold the highest standards in breed certification, health testing, and medical documentation to ensure every puppy meets our rigorous benchmarks.

AKC Registered

Every Heritage Hill puppy is registered with the American Kennel Club, providing verified documentation of lineage, breed standards, and pedigree. Our breeding stock meets or exceeds AKC conformation guidelines.

Comprehensive Health Testing

All of our breeding dogs undergo thorough health screenings including hip and elbow evaluations, eye exams, genetic testing for MDR1 and HSF4, and cardiac assessments. We test to ensure the healthiest possible puppies.

Health Guarantee

Every puppy from Heritage Hill Aussies comes with a written health guarantee, complete vaccination records, deworming history, and full medical documentation. We stand behind the health of our dogs with confidence.
