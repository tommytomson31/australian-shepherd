Common Questions

How do I reserve a puppy?

We require a non-refundable deposit to reserve your puppy. The remaining balance is due before pickup. Contact us for current pricing and availability.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that welcoming a new family member is a significant investment. Contact us to discuss flexible payment options.

What health guarantees do you offer?

Every puppy comes with a comprehensive health guarantee, complete vaccination records, and AKC registration. We stand behind the health of our puppies.

How do I know my puppy is healthy?

Every puppy comes with a complete veterinary health certificate, up-to-date vaccination records, and a written health guarantee. We provide full transparency with all medical documentation.
